# Gaia-Inspired Smoke Deck

This repo-authored sample is based on the bright, spacious visual direction people often expect from Marp's popular built-in Gaia theme.

- Large headline scale
- Clean white stage
- Strong accent bands
- Presenter-friendly closing slide

---

## Room To Read

Gaia-style decks are useful smoke fixtures because they emphasize:

- large readable typography
- simple, high-contrast surfaces
- slide-to-slide rhythm instead of ornamental styling
